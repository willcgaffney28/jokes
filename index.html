Our Mission

The Alliance for Nepo Baby Dignity (ANBD) exists to counter the harmful narrative that young professionals who secured roles at top banks, hedge funds, and private equity firms through family connections are somehow "less deserving" of respect.

We believe that having a parent who played golf with the managing director, or whose last name opens doors that remain closed to others, should not disqualify someone from basic human dignity. Nepo babies have feelings too.
